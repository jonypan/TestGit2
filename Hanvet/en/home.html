About Traphaco
With more than 44 years of history, Traphaco has brought the traditional medicine of Vietnam to the new height by the use of modern technology, the creativeness of Vietnamese people and its responsibility to serve community health. The company is moving on the path of "Green Health".
The leaders of Traphaco - the pioneers in building new business strategies and applying advanced management tools for the company.
Devoting to the society the products & services of the age and traditional values to improve the quality of life.
As the leading brand of Vietnam's pharmaceutical industry, Traphaco always considers community interests as the motivation for all of its activities. Traphaco has positively contributed to the common development of the entire society. The company has won many prestigious awards and titles.
